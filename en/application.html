If you decided to apply for the job as an English teacher in China, here are common steps to do it

Step 1

Record a self-introduction or teaching video with some information about yourself, your interests, experience and send us.

Step 2

Write and send us your resume (CV) with some information of yourself (education, experience, personal qualities), with your contacts and photo in Word MS or PDF form.

Step 3

We search a school for you. In some cases employer may ask for an interview with you through Skype or Wechat.

Step 4

After successful acceptance of your candidacy, the employer sends a contract for you to sign. Then we discuss our future cooperation or other details.

Step 5

You apply for a visa and buy tickets to China where we meet you and arrange your first days of a new life.

So, don't hesitate and make your first steps with Winter!
